🍳 Meal by Meal

Breakfast — keep & build

2 eggs = ~12g protein. Add Greek yogurt 0% fat or protein shake to hit 25–30g. Coffee is fine.

Lunch — your biggest lever

Current: Large rice or noodles, low protein, 1–2pm → goes to fat storage.
Target: Half carb portion + 100–150g cooked protein + as much veg as possible.

Dinner — mostly keep it

Your wife's dinners are good. Keep protein and veg. Just eat no later than 7pm.

Evening snacking — break this habit

Phase 1 (Wk 1–4): Swap chips/chocolate/ice cream → Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, or protein shake.

Phase 2 (Wk 5+): Hard kitchen close at 9pm. Water or herbal tea only after.

Supplements worth taking

  • Protein powder — whey or plant-based, to hit 130g daily
  • Creatine monohydrate — 5g every day, best evidence for strength and body comp
  • Vitamin D3 — 2000–4000 IU, Calgary winters + indoor job
  • Magnesium glycinate — 300–400mg before bed, improves sleep quality measurably
