🍳 Meal by Meal

Breakfast — keep & build on it

2 eggs = ~12g protein. Add Greek yogurt 0% fat (15–17g) OR protein shake. Target 25–30g first thing. Coffee is fine.

Lunch — your biggest lever

Current: Large rice or noodles, low protein, eaten 1–2pm → insulin at weakest, goes straight to fat storage.
Target: Half the carb portion + 100–150g cooked protein (chicken / beef / fish / tofu) + as much veg as possible. Same foods, better ratio.

Dinner — mostly keep it

Your wife's dinners are well-structured. Keep protein and veg focus. One change: eat no later than 7pm. The later you eat the more it disrupts overnight fat burning.

Evening snacking — the habit to break

Phase 1 (Wk 1–4) Swap: Replace chips / chocolate / ice cream with Greek yogurt + honey, cottage cheese, or protein shake.

Phase 2 (Wk 5+) Cut off: Hard kitchen close at 9pm. Water, herbal tea, sparkling water only after that.

Supplements worth taking

  • Protein powder — whey or plant-based, to hit your 130g target
  • Creatine monohydrate — 5g every day. Best evidence-based supplement for strength and body composition
  • Vitamin D3 — 2000–4000 IU daily. Calgary winters plus indoor job = deficiency risk
  • Magnesium glycinate — 300–400mg before bed. Meaningfully improves sleep quality

📆 Weekly Training Split

Tuesday — Upper Body Strength
  • Bench Press: 3×5 (heavier than your old 8-rep weight)
  • Overhead Press: 3×10 — your primary shoulder builder
  • Bent-Over Row: 3×8 | Bicep Curls: 2×12
  • Face Pulls or Rear Delt Flies: 3×15 — do not skip these
Wednesday — Badminton (1 hour, keep exactly as is)
Thursday or Friday — Lower Body + Core
  • Squat: 3×8 | Romanian Deadlift: 3×10 | Lunges: 2×12 per leg
  • Plank: 3×45s | Ab wheel or hanging leg raises: 3×12
Saturday — Full Body + 30 min Cardio
  • Deadlift: 3×5 (heavier, lower rep) | Arnold Press: 3×10
  • Pull-ups or Lat Pulldown: 3×8–10 | Cardio: alternate run and bike weekly
📌 Progressive overload rule: Every 2 weeks add 5 lbs to main lifts OR add 1 rep per set. The Coach tab tracks this automatically.

😴 Sleep Protocol

🎯 Goal: In bed 11:30pm → up 7:30am = 7.5 hours. Move bedtime 15 min earlier each week from your current 1am.
  • 9:30 PM: Hard stop on all work — no email, no Slack, nothing
  • 9:30–11:30 PM: Low stimulation — no doomscrolling social media
  • Phone: Face down or charging outside the bedroom, all notifications off
  • Magnesium glycinate 300mg before bed — meaningfully improves deep sleep quality
